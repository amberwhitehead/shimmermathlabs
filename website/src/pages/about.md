_Shimmer Math Labs_ is a small software development company that specializes in
high quality mathematical programming. We build amazing things to make your
reality more interesting.

Amber is a professional software developer and the founder of _Shimmer Math
Labs_. She has been programming since she was 8 and trying to make computers
do amazing things ever since. Amber received her PhD in computer science from
the University of California, Santa Cruz. Most recently she has worked at
NVIDIA® trying to make deep learning inference go faster. Amber's interests
include math, AI, performance optimization, audio algorithms, typesetting, and
building software. She lives in Tacoma, Washington.

Amber has used the name Nathan in the past.
